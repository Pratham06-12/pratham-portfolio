## Problem Statement

With thousands of titles added annually, streaming platforms like Prime Video need to monitor their catalog's diversity, audience targeting, and geographical reach. Understanding these trends helps in making better content acquisition and production decisions.

The goal was to transform raw CSV data into a comprehensive visual overview that identifies the platform's core content strengths and library gaps.

## Key Insights

- **TV Shows Dominate**: 80.8% of the catalog consists of TV Shows, indicating a strong focus on episodic content and series retention.
- **Global Reach**: While the USA and India are the primary content production hubs, the platform shows significant diversification in European and Asian markets.
- **Audience Targeting**: The "13+" rating is the most prevalent, showing that Prime Video's primary niche is the teenage and young adult demographic.
- **Content surge**: Over 60% of the platform's library was produced between 2015 and 2021, highlighting the rapid expansion in the streaming era.

## Tools Used

- **Power BI Desktop**: For dashboard creation and complex visualizations.
- **Power Query**: Performed advanced ETL to clean inconsistent genre names and handle missing production years.
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**: Created custom measures for content penetration and year-over-year growth calculations.
- **Bing Maps Integration**: Developed a geographic heatmap for production country density.

## Result

Delivered an interactive dashboard that allows stakeholders to filter content by year, rating, and genre simultaneously, providing a 360-degree view of the platform's library health.
